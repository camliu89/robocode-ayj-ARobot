Welcome to the Robocode-ayj-Arobot project

The project is dedicated to improve the performance of ARobot to compete in a Robocode tournament.

Movement

ARobot moves according to the opponent's behavior. First it will sit at his initial spot. When an enemy's bullet is fired, ARobot will try to avoid it by moving left or right. That is why ARobot will have its gun pointing to the enemy, but having the tank in a position so that it is ready to move left or right. If ARobot is hit by a bullet, it will fall back by 100 pixels.

Firing

ARobot fires when it scans an enemy, but the firepower depends on the enemy robot distance. The closest the enemy is, the highest the firepower.

Targeting

ARobot will point with its gun only one enemy in the battlefield. If it loses the track of its enemy, then it will move forward 10 pixels and turn 10 degrees its radar to look for another enemy.

Vulnerabilites

ARobot will fire at every turn. It does not predict the movement of the enemy robot. Therefore it will lose energy at every turn. It does not also attempt to avoid any near robot, making collisions happen if possible.
